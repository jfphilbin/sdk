Welcome to the Open DICOMweb (ODW) SDK

The ODW SDK is an open source toolkit for developing Web-based applications for medical imaging. It conforms to the DICOM Standard. The toolkit's goals, in order, are:

  1. Correctness
  2. Simplicity
  3. Readability, and
  4. Efficiency

Authors and Contributors

Jim Philbin (@jfphilbin) is the creator of the toolkit. Anyone interested in contributing is welcome.
